Project Overview

This autonomous robotics system represents 6 months of intensive research, design iteration, and precision manufacturing. Built from the ground up with a focus on intelligent navigation and advanced object manipulation capabilities.

As the lead mechanical engineer on this project, I spearheaded every aspect of development:

  • Conceptual design and rapid prototyping methodologies
  • Advanced 3D modeling with finite element analysis
  • Strategic material selection and component optimization
  • Precision CNC machining and additive manufacturing
  • Comprehensive system integration and performance validation

Advanced Materials

6061 Aluminum chassis with carbon fiber reinforcement for optimal strength-to-weight ratio

Precision Actuation

Quad DC motor system with high-resolution encoder feedback loops

Computing Power

Raspberry Pi 4 brain with Arduino Mega co-processor architecture

Sensor Fusion

LIDAR mapping, IMU stabilization, and computer vision processing
